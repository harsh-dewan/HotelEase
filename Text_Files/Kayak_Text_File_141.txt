Freebies This stay includes Wi-Fi and Parking for free The Drake Inn in Alberta, Canada is owned by some of the locals in the area. The hotel offers incredible views from private balconies and amenities to help all guests relax. With a location that is surrounded by the Canadian Rockies and as staff comprised of locals who understand the value of the place, this hotel choice is one that delivers high value to its guests. All rooms have been recently upgraded. There are 26 in total. There are rooms that are Mountain View with one bed or two. There is also a Mountain View Creekside Suite that sleeps up to six people. There are two balconies, a coffee maker, a mini-fridge, a microwave, free WiFi, cable TV, and separate living space in the suite. Guests can also park for free on the property. Guests at The Drake Inn can eat at The Drake Publichouse. This is the on-property restaurant. There is another restaurant owned by these same owners a few blocks from the hotel called The Wood Restaurant & Lounge. Other options include Tapa Restaurant, The Trough Dining Co, and Communitea Café. There are many activities in the surrounding area that travelers can participate in. Some that are popular include rafting, mountain biking, and hiking when it’s warm. There’s golfing nearby too. When it’s cold and snowy there are plenty of other activities as well, including cross-country skiing, downhill skiing, and snowboarding. Other attractions include Canmore Caverns, Cross Zee Ranch, and Canmore Golf and Curling Club.
