Freebies This stay includes Wi-Fi for free Situated within a block from rue Sainte Catherine and within minutes from the Champ-de-Mars metro station, Hôtel CHROME Montréal Centre-ville provides comfortable accommodation for guests. The hotel offers 123 non-smoking guestrooms spread over 8 floors. Each room is outfitted with tea/coffeemakers, alarm clocks, high-speed internet, ironing board and flat-screen TVs with cable. The executive suite improves on this with a dining room, a fully equipped kitchen, dedicated workspace and added living space. Dining options at the hotel include Grill Le 215, a casual on-site restaurant and bar that is situated on the main floor. It offers a complete menu for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Guest can enjoy their meals indoors or while catching some sun on the outdoor terrace (which is only open during the summer). In addition, the hotel has a business center where guests can send and receive faxes, print documents and make copies. It also houses a banquet hall and conference rooms that can be reserved for a range of events. Other hotel amenities include convenient parking, a children park, early check-ins and late-checkouts and free newspaper (national and local). Nestled in the heart of Montreal, the hotel is situated close to many of the local attractions. It’s within a quick 10-minute walk to Notre Dame Basilica or the Old Port of Montreal. Set within a seven-minute walk, the Underground City provides a spot for shopping enthusiasts to get their fix. Other nearby attractions include the Bonsecours Market and the University of Quebec-Montreal.
