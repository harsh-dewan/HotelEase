This downtown hotel offers complimentary breakfast for all guests and free WiFi throughout the building and is a short walk from the Museum of Pop Culture and the Seattle Waterfront. There are a total of 72 rooms available at this hotel, including a limited number of adjoining rooms for families and groups. The lobby features a fireplace and lounge area for guest enjoyment.
