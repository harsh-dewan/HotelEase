The hotel is designed in the style of an old Roman house. The location of the hotel gives the best of the city in downtown Vancouver, as well as, the parks and beautiful nature of the Burnaby Mountains. The hotel’s 100 rooms all have plush beds and amenities that make for a comfortable stay. There are traditional rooms with just one bed for those traveling alone for business or as a couple. There are also traditional rooms that have two beds for those traveling with others. A king suite gives more space and a separated living area. The corner king room has a microwave and a refrigerator so it’s great for longer stays. There is free WiFi, steam shower heads, coffeemakers, large TVs, and desks in all of the rooms. Atrium Inn Vancouver serves a free breakfast in the mornings. For other meals, there’s the PressBox Pub that is on site. It’s a good spot for having drinks, some food, and watching a game. Downtown Vancouver isn’t far away with its plethora of restaurants, bars, cafes, and more. The hotel has a free shuttle service that can be booked to get anywhere in town. The hotel has a fitness center for getting in those workouts. Hastings Park is right across from the front of the inn. Guests can venture into Vancouver more, or walk to some area attractions. Playland Amusement Park, Hastings Racecourse, Empire Fields, Park and Tilford Gardens, and the Port of Vancouver are all within walking distance.
