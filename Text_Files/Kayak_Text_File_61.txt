Freebies This stay includes Wi-Fi and Parking for free The Ambassadeur Hotel is ideally located near the Domaine de Maizerets in Quebec. There is an indoor pool as well as a separate children's pool area. Plus, the hotel has a nightclub, bar area, and a restaurant. Breakfast is available each morning. Guests can make use of the health club and the garden area. Plus, there is are spa services available on site. The front desk area is open all day and night, and the staff members can offer a safe for storing valuables. There are 145 guestrooms which are all smoke-free. Travelers can choose between queen-sized or double beds, and infant beds or rollaway beds can be provided for a small surcharge if needed. The rooms contain a refrigerator for guest convenience, and they are outfitted with complimentary WiFi. Connecting rooms are available for those traveling in larger parties. The housekeeping staff attends to the rooms on a daily basis to ensure that everything is stocked and tidy. There are plenty of attractions and landmarks in the vicinity of the hotel, and several of them are within walking distance. The Domaine de Maizerets is about a 20-minute walk from the hotel, and the L'Enfant-Jesus Hospital is about a 30-minute walk. Guests can also reach the Cegep Limoilou and the Naval Museum of Quebec on foot. Other area attractions include the Agora Port de Quebec, the Parliament building, and Montmorency Falls. The nearest airport is the Jean Lesage International Airport, which is about 17 minutes away from the hotel via car.
