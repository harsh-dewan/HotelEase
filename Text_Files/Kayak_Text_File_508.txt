Pantages Hotel Downtown Toronto is a boutique hotel located in the heart of downtown Toronto, just steps from many of the best sights in the city. This hotel sits in the heart of one of the most vibrant neighborhoods in the city, offering easy access to theatres, shopping, and Yonge Street, the longest street in Canada. Guests can visit popular attractions like Toronto Eaton Centre, St. Lawrence Market, the Royal Alexandria Theatre, and the Hockey Hall of Fame. The hotel provides a dedicated meeting and events space for upscale events in downtown Toronto. Complimentary WiFi is available throughout the building. Pantages Hotel Downtown Toronto features its own on-site restaurant and lounge, which provides room service to guests. There is a fitness center and a business center at this hotel, along with a rainforest relaxation room. This four-star hotel offers a total of 89 guest rooms with 24-hour front desk services. The business center and fitness center are also available 24 hours a day. Breakfast is available at the hotel, which also provides daily housekeeping and a garden and terrace for guest use. The guest rooms at this hotel include floor-to-ceiling windows, rainfall showers, a dedicated work desk, flat-screen TVs with premium channels, premium bath amenities, and other modern conveniences. Select suites include a kitchenette and washer and dryer. Guests can choose from deluxe rooms, city rooms, standard rooms, and a number of suites at this non-smoking hotel, which is steps from public transit and just 20 minutes from Toronto International Airport. Pets are not allowed at this hotel.
