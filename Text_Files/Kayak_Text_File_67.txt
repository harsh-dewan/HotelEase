Freebies This stay includes Wi-Fi and Parking for free Those traveling to Canmore in Alberta, Canada will find a beautiful backdrop when staying at Canmore Inn & Suites. This hotel is known to be kid-friendly and have warm, friendly service. It has a slew of amenities to please different kinds of guests, including free parking, an indoor pool with water slide, and free high-speed wireless Internet. The hotel houses 189 rooms. Guests can choose from economy level rooms or deluxe. There are also accessible rooms available and the Carriage suite with a view of the railway. The suite has a separate bedroom with a king bed and one with a queen. There is also a sofa bed and two full bathrooms. One of the tubs is jetted. Guests who choose this suite will also find a kitchenette where some small meals can be made. There are also family rooms and the Pullman suite. Every morning the Canmore Inn & Suites has a breakfast buffet where guests can pay a small fee to partake. The hotel has an on-site restaurant as well. Spice Hut is an Eastern Indian restaurant that serves curries, vindaloo, and more spicy cuisine. Not far from the property are some other dining choices, such as Crazy Weed for Asian, Tapas Restaurant, and Communitea Café. Canmore Inn & Suites is close to some interesting attractions guests might like to visit. The Canmore Museum and Geoscience Centre is 11 minutes away on foot. There is the Canmore Golf and Curling Club not much further than that. Some fun family time can be spent at Yamnuska Mountain Adventures and Canmore Caverns.
