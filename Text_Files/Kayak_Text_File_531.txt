Freebies This stay includes Wi-Fi and Parking for free Sandy Beach Motel offers guests thoughtful beachfront lodgings in one of southern British Columbia’s most scenic destination spots. Its rooms, amenities, and location make this motel a popular option for couples, families, and groups, particularly those who love the outdoors. Located on the banks of the Osoyoos River in Osoyoos, Sandy Beach Motel is close to many of the area’s top attractions while being less than an hour’s drive from Penticton Airport. While staying here, guests can enjoy easy access to sites like Columbia Mountains, Osoyoos Art Gallery, Rattlesnake Canyon amusement park, and Nk’mip Desert & Heritage Center, Accommodations are elegantly furnished and inviting. Guests may choose Studios with one queen bed and one double sofa bed, lakefront One-Bedroom Deluxe Rooms with one king and one sofa bed, or Two-Bedroom Cottages with one queen bed, two twin beds, and one double sofa bed. All rooms feature private bathrooms, patios, free Wi-Fi, kitchens, and flat-screen TVs. While guests can easily prepare their own meals in the comfort of their rooms, the hotel also provides outdoor barbecue grills and picnic seating for added fun. Many of the area’s best-loved restaurants and bars are also close by. At Sandy Beach Motel, guests can enjoy a range of activities to occupy free time including tennis, kayaking, volleyball, and a playground for the kids. Laundry facilities are also available, as is a business center for anyone needing to send or receive documents.
